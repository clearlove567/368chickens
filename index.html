About 368 Chickens

üéÆ Game Overview

368 Chickens is a minimalist, browser-first puzzle-strategy title created by indie developer pompomsheep. Released in January 2025, this delightfully simple game went viral within a single week, surpassing 500,000 unique players and spawning dozens of unauthorized clones across the internet.

The premise is beautifully simple: start with 368 cartoon chickens and rescue every last one by strategically lining up identical birds in groups of three on a 6√ó6 grid. What started as a small indie project became a global phenomenon, with TikTok hashtags like #368ChickensGame accumulating over 46 million views in just two months.
